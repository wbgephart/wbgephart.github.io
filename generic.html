About Me

Hey there! My name is Will Gephart and I am a data analyst currently living in Indianapolis, IN and working for the Purdue Research Foundation. I grew up in Indiana and earned my Bachelor's of Science in Kinesiology and Sport Science at Purdue University. After graduating, I began working as a health coach and over time developed a curiosity for helping individuals succeed through statistics and data. I obtained my Master's Degree in Data Analytics at the University of Mississippi in May of 2023. I am also continuing to build my skills through projects outside of work to develop a diverse skillset. In the time I have outside of work I love spending time with my wife, Elle, and our dog, Arlo! Some of our favorite things to do include being outside, spending time with friends and family, and playing board games. If you have any questions about me or my work, feel free to send me an email at wbgephart@gmail.com.
